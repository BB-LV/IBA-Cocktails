50mL Vodka Vanilla
15mL Elderflower Cordial
15mL Fresh Lime Juice
10mL Monin Honey Syrup
2 Thinly Sliced Chili Peppers
Garnish
Garnish with A Red Chili Pepper
Method
Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker. Shake well with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Spritz

Reveal Method
90mL Prosecco
60mL Aperol
A Splash of Soda Water
Garnish
Garnish with An Orange Slice
Method
Pour all ingredients into a wine glass filled with ice and stir gently. *Note* Other Variations include subsituting Campari, Cynar or Select for Aperol.

Suffering Bastard

Reveal Method
30mL Cognac or Brandy
30mL Gin
15mL Fresh Lime Juice
2 Dashes of Angostura Bitters
Top with Ginger Beer
Garnish
Garnish with Minth Sprigs. Optional: Orange Slice
Method
Pour all ingredients(Except ginger beer) into a cocktail shaker. Shake well with ice and pour(unstrained) into a collins glass or a 'S. Bastard Mug'. Top with ginger beer.

Tipperary

Reveal Method
50mL Irish Whisky
25mL Sweet Red Vermouth
15mL Green Chartreuse
2 Dashes Angostura Bitters
Garnish
Garnish with an Orange Slice
Method
Pour all ingredients into a mixing glass with ice cubes. Stir well. Strain into a chilled martini glass.

Tommy's Margarita

Reveal Method
60mL Tequila Reposado(100% Agave)
30mL Fresh Lime Juice(Persiano)
30mL Agave Syrup
Garnish
Garnish with A Slice of Lime
Method
Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker. Shake well with ice and strain into a chilled rocks glass with ice.

VE.N.To

Reveal Method
45mL White Smooth Grappa
15mL Chamomile Cordial
22.5mL Fresh Lemon Juice
15mL Honey Mix(Syrup)
10mL Egg White(Optional)
Garnish
Garnish with Lemon Zest and White Grapes
Method
Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker. Shake vigorously with ice and strain into a chilled small tumbler glass with ice.

Yellow Bird

Reveal Method
30mL White Rum
15mL Galliano
15mL Triple Sec
15mL Fresh Lime Juice
Garnish
Presented Neat (No Garnish)
Method
Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker. Shake well with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
