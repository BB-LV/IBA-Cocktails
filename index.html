Brandy Crusta

Reveal Method
52.5mL Brandy
7.5mL Maraschino Luxardo
15mL Fresh Lemon Juice
1 Bar Spoon Curacao
1 Bar Spoon Simple Syrup
2 Dashes Aromatic Bitters
oZ Conversion is an Approximation
Garnish
Rub a slice of orange (or lemon) around the rim of the glass and dip it in pulverized white sugar, so that the sugar will adhere to the edge of the glass. Carefully curling place the orange/lemon peel around the inside of the glass.
Method
Mix together all ingredients with ice cubes in a mixing glass and strain into prepared slim cocktail glass

Old Fashioned

Reveal Method

Few Dashes Water
oZ Conversion is an Approximation
Garnish
Garnish with an orange slice or zest and a cocktail cherry.
Method

Ramos Fizz

Reveal Method
45mL Gin
15mL Fresh Lime Juice
15mL Fresh Lemon Juice
30mL Sugar Syrup
60mL Cream
30mL Egg White
3 Dashes of Orange Flower Water
2 Drops of Vanilla Extract
Top with Soda Water
oZ Conversion is an Approximation
Garnish
Presented Neat (No Garnish)
Method
Pour all ingredients(except soda water) into a cocktail shaker. Shake well with ice for two minutes and double strain into a glass. Pour the drink back into the shaker and hard shake without ice for one minute. Strain into a highball glass and top with soda water.

Sazerac

Reveal Method
50mL Cognac
10mL Absinthe
1 Sugar Cube
2 Dashes Peychaud's Bitters
oZ Conversion is an Approximation
Garnish
Garnish with a Lemon Zest
Method
Rinse a chilled old fashioned glass with the absinthe, add crushed ice and set it aside to chill. Stir the remaining ingredients over ice in a mixing glass. Discard the ice and Absinthe in the chilled glass and strain cocktail into the chilled glass.
